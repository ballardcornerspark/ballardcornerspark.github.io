About

Welcome to the website of the Friends of Ballard Corners Park. We are a community group helping to steer the acquisition, design, development, construction, and maintenance of a neighborhood park on two lots between NW 62nd and 63rd Streets along the west side of 17 Ave NW in the Ballard neighborhood of Seattle, Washington. We need your help in realizing the goal of creating a fun place for play, gathering, and relaxation in the heart of Ballard.Want to see our alternative proposed designs? The preferred alternative? Our Master Plan Report which is a comprehensive review of the process to date?

Check out our new pictures of our Easter egg hunt, the first community meeting, fence knock down party, and everything 2006!
