Merchandise

Our shopping cart and checkout are powered by PayPal. We accept payment via PayPal account, all major credit cards and contact us personally for other payment methods or to arrange for bulk orders or fundraising activities.

All proceeds from merchandise will go to the park, although our prices are set to cover our costs and spread the word!

We make every effort to keep our t-shirts and buttons in stock and will contact you should there be any issue – so please provide valid contact information!
