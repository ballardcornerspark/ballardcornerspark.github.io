Column Bases

I dropped by to visit with Nathan Arnold (Nathan the mason) today.  He was riding a steep learning curve and expressing frustration about some challenges he was having.  He was having a hard time with the compound angles on the column bases and the taper.  He was also having a hard time getting the forms to release without taking some of the concrete with the forms.  He was going to experiment with plastic.
