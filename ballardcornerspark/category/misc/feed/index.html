Compass USA is looking for families + homes in the Ballard area interested in hosting Japanese students for a short time this summer. Fifteen (15) junior high school boys and girls (ages 12-15) come to our community from a private school in Japan to learn about our customs and culture by staying with Ballard-area families. During their stay, the students are involved in a program of classes and weekday field trips.

  • Families who would welcome an international experience this summer are needed in your community NOW!
  • Learn about Japan and the Japanese culture! Develop lifetime friendships!
  • Host a Japanese junior high school students for 3 weeks in your home this summer.
  • This is an excellent opportunity to introduce your family to a beautiful, new experience you won’t forget!

July 14 through August 3, 2009
