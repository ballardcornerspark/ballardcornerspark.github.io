Native Plant Sale – Saturday, May 5th

Go Natives!, a Shoreline based native plant nursery, will be holding a plant sale on Saturday, May 5th at the corner of NW 17th and 63rd NW in Ballard (Ballard Corners Park!!!). The sale runs from 10am and till 5pm. 10% of their gross sales will be donated to Groundswell NW. Groundswell NW will provide advice on conversion of yards to using less water and lower pesticides, attracting wildlife, and providing esthetic alternatives that require less maintenance.

Information on Go Natives (sales dates and inventory) as well as links to helpful sources of plant information are available at www.gonatives.com

Let Don know what plants you want by emailing Don Norman at gonatives@gmail.com before the sale and they will have the plants at the sale for you! If you are interested in a site visit consultation, that can also be included in the donation to Groundswell NW.
