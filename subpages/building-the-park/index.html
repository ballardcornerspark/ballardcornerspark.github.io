Building The Park (not used, don’t delete though)

The north lot of our park (6219 17th Ave NW) has been purchased by the city of Seattle and is essentially a vacant lot.

The city is attempting to purchase the the south lot (1702 NW 62 St) with its residence, which has been unoccupied for approximately 15 years.

A small and simple grant from the City of Seattle’s Department of Neighborhoods for $15,000 is being used to design and develop a plan for the park space.

Barker Landscape Architects has been selected to design the park with input from three community meetings and the guidance of the steering committee.

Seattle Parks and Recreation through its Pro Parks Levy worked to acquire the property and will be responsible for maintenance. They are helping to guide the process. $150,000 has been pledged by the Pro Parks Levy toward construction costs. We will be seeking additional monies through grants to assist us in building the park that the community wants on this property.

We need your input and contributions to make this park a reality. Please help us! Come to our community meetings or donate to our project (volunteer labor and goods).
